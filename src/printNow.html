Reprographics & Print Services

CES&R offers imaging, copying and finishing capabilities for all your projects big or small, combining superior customer service and the attention to detail you deserve. Our certified team will produce quality construction plans & detailed visuals exactly when and how you want them. We offer a broad variety of reprographic services, including: large format imaging and scanning, small format color and B&W imaging, comprehensive finishing services.

LARGE FORMAT

Whether you need drawings, plan sheets, presentation boards or a wall hanging, our project-oriented quality control process allows us to provide the care and attention needed to ensure your complete satisfaction. Our large-format imaging capabilities include:
  • Color Imaging up to 60″ wide on multiple media types
  • Large format digital imaging on bond, mylar or vellum
  • Large format color copying
  • Scanning up to 54″ wide
  • Laminating & Encapsulating
  • Dry Mounting & Edging
  • Specialty Media
  • Binding & Folding

SMALL FORMAT

At CES&R, we specialize in doing custom work of the highest quality, according to your exact specifications. We image directly from electronic files or hard copy to produce your books, booklets, reports, proposals, newsletters and manuals. Color imaging for entire documents, or color inserts in black & white books.
  • Wide selection of stock including white, color, card stock, glossy and various specialty papers
  • Comprehensive choices of binding systems, including 3-ring binders with cover and spine inserts, GBC, coil (spiral), twin loop, tape, screwpost (with or without tape), Acco and staple
  • Imaging on cover/card stock (choice of colors)
  • High speed digital imaging or copying in B&W
  • Folding, tabs and more

FINISHINGS

CES&R offers a broad variety of finishing services — including binding, laminating and dry mounting — to meet all your needs. Options include:
  • Comprehensive choices in binding systems, from 3-ring binders with cover and spine inserts, to GBC, coil (spiral), twin loop, tape, screwpost (with or without tape), Acco and staple binding
  • Satin or glossy laminates over matte, satin or coated (glossy) paper to protect images and create the exact look you seek
  • Dry mounting with your choice of backing and edging to meet your requirements for durability, rigidity, weight and ease of presentation

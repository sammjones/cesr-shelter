Printer Repair Service


With over 25 years of experience, our printer repair technicians at CES&R can handle any problems you could be having with your printer. We service and repair multifunctioning copiers, wide-format printers, and plotter printers in Utah. Our repairs are performed on-site, meaning our technicians will come to you if you need assistance. We understand that businesses operate on a budget, so our estimates are free, and we repair equipment on-site to keep your business’s costs to a minimum.

Our relationship with our customers is our top priority. You’ll appreciate the honest service you receive. That’s because CES&R service technicians are committed to getting your machines running again. They’ll never try to sell you parts or extras you don’t need. We are happy to provide our customers with any other information needed, for you to make an informed decision about your copier repair needs.

EMAIL SERVICE

CES&R provides preventative equipment maintenance, to stop problems before they happen, as well as repairs covered by factory warranties. We provide printer repair services for a variety of issues, including, but not limited to:

  • On-site repair
  • Print quality issues
  • Troubleshooting and assessment
  • Preventative maintenance
  • Factory warranty repairs

CES&R is an authorized reseller and repair service provider for a variety of major printer brands, including Canon, KIP, and Kyrocera Copystar. Our experience with major printer brands helps us provide you with the best quality servicing and maintenance for your major brand printer. We offer our customers contracts for printer support and repair, to ensure your equipment is always in top shape. All printer and copier service techs are OCCEA (Organic Certification Clean Equipment Affidavit) trained.

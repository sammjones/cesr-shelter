SERVICE DEPARTMENT


With over 25 years of experience CES&R has a staff of knowledgeable technicians and support personnel that can handle any problems you may be facing. We provide preventative equipment maintenance on multifunction copiers, wide-format printers, and plotters, to stop problems before they happen, as well as repairs covered by factory warranties. Repairs are performed on-site, meaning our technicians will come to you.

Our relationship with our customers is our top priority. You’ll appreciate the honest service you receive. CES&R will provide you with any information needed for you to make an informed decision about your repair needs. We understand that businesses operate on a budget, so our team will never try to sell you parts or extras you don’t need.

   EMAIL SERVICE

CES&R is an authorized reseller and repair service provider for a variety of major printer brands, including Canon, KIP, and Kyocera. Our experience helps us provide you with the best quality servicing and maintenance for your major brand printer. We offer our customers contracts for printer support and repair, to ensure your equipment is always in top shape. All printer and copier service techs are OCCEA (Organic Certification Clean Equipment Affidavit) trained.
